# 05-retrospectives

Purpose:
Reflect on progress, process, and outcomes over a period of work.

Examples of notes:
- End-of-sprint reflections on what went well and what did not.
- Post-mortem for a major issue or incident.
- Monthly review of learning and productivity on this project.

Structure:
YYYY-MM-DD-title.md

Example:
- 2026-01-17-centralized-retro.md
